Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Larder · Mise en Go (“the app”) is developed and published by Larder · Mise en Go, a sole trader registered in the Netherlands (KVK: ). This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

What data we collect

Larder · Mise en Go is designed to store your data locally on your device. The app collects and stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Pantry items and stock levels you enter
  • Recipes you import or add manually
  • Fresh This Week items
  • App preferences and settings

Backend identifier

To manage the included recipe import allowance, the app generates an anonymous device identifier and sends it to our server each time you import a recipe. This identifier is a randomly generated string tied to your device installation. It is not linked to your name, email address, or any personal information. It is stored on our server solely to track the number of imports used.

Google Drive backup

If you choose to enable the optional backup feature, your pantry and recipe data is exported as a single JSON file and saved to your personal Google Drive account. This data is stored under your own Google account and is not accessible to Nuttig. You may delete this backup at any time from your Google Drive.

AI recipe restructuring

When you import a recipe, the recipe text is sent to Anthropic’s API (claude.ai) to restructure it into mise en place format. This means the text of the recipe is processed by Anthropic’s servers. No personal information, pantry data, or account data is included in this request. Anthropic’s privacy policy applies to this processing: anthropic.com/privacy.

By using the recipe import feature, you consent to recipe text being sent to Anthropic for processing.

Analytics and tracking

The app does not collect analytics, crash reports, usage statistics, or any other telemetry. We do not track how you use the app.

Advertising

The app contains no advertising of any kind.

Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic API — recipe text restructuring (see above)
  • Google Drive API — optional backup only, under your own Google account
  • Google Play — app distribution; Google’s own privacy policy applies to purchases

Children’s privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@misego.app.
